---
name: AI search entry selection
description: How entries should be selected when running AI search in the journal
type: feedback
---

When limiting entries for AI search, always include entries matched by keyword expansion — don't discard them in favor of recency alone.

**Why:** The user explicitly asked for keyword-matched entries to be included; recency alone may miss the most relevant entries.

**How to apply:** When capping entries for the AI search request, combine or prioritize keyword-filtered results rather than slicing purely by date.
